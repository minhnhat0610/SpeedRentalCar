we support our customers

24 hours road-side assistance

Let us be there when you need help most. Speed Car is committed to offering top notch service from picking up your car, to dropping it off. With your Speed Car rental, you receive free 24-hour road-side assistance. No hassle, no paperwork, and no need to ask, we’ve got you covered automatically.

***24 Hour Road-Side Assistance provided via a partnership with AAA

customer loyalty program

At Speed Car rentals, each customer is like family. Rented with us before? Enjoy great discounts as part of our customer loyalty program. Sign up to become a loyalty program member at booking or ask your local agent how to sign up. Our loyalty customers receive on average a savings of $50 per rental. The more your rent with us, the more you save. Start earning your loyalty rewards today!

***Program subject to change. Savings based on a national average.
